PROJECT INSIGHTS

1. Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category, driven by high-value products like Laptop, Mobile, and Monitor.

2. Laptop remains the top-selling product by revenue, followed by Mobile and Tablet.

3. Accessories and Clothing categories show frequent purchases but lower revenue per order, indicating low-cost, high-volume sales.

4. Furniture category contributes moderate revenue with products like Desk and Chair, showing potential for growth.

5. Stationery category has the highest quantity sold (Pens, Notebooks) but very low revenue, highlighting low-price bulk sales.

6. Monthly sales show a consistent increasing trend from January to August, indicating business growth over time.

7. July and August recorded the highest sales due to a wider variety of products and increased transactions.

8. Average order value is balanced, with a mix of high-ticket and low-ticket products.

9. High-value electronics products contribute the majority of total revenue despite fewer quantities sold.

10. Product diversification (Electronics, Clothing, Furniture, Stationery) helps maintain steady overall sales performance.
